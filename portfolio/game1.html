Changeling VR

A VR mystery adventure game

I worked on Changeling VR, a 3D virtual reality game where players explore the minds of family members, each with unique perspectives, personalities, and challenges. As Lead Level Designer, I led both technical development and bridged the gap between mechanics, narrative, and art, ensuring all elements aligned with the game's vision.


I overhauled five key sections of the Father's level, integrating distinct mechanics, art styles, and narrative threads. My responsibilities included designing complex gameplay systems, establishing technical architecture, and creating unique, engaging levels that delivered a cohesive narrative, artistic, and gameplay experience. I implemented key systems such as gear combination puzzles, electrical wiring mechanics, pipe orientation challenges, a dynamic bike-building and customization system, and more. Additionally, I helped polish dynamic destruction features and ensured all technical elements were optimized. The game was built in Unreal Engine 4 using Blueprint visual scripting and is now available on Steam.
