Headless Harry

A fast paced 2D platformer

Headless Harry is a humorous and engaging game where players take on the role of Harry, a headless chicken on a daring escape from Chef de Blob's restaurant. As the Combat Lead, I was responsible for designing and implementing a wide range of features that shaped the core gameplay experience.

I developed and designed all of the player's abilities, including implementing features like gliding, wall jumping, and ground pounding. Additionally, I designed diverse enemy behaviors and abilities, introduced a variety of enemy types, and integrated challenging level obstacles to enhance the gameplay. I worked to fine-tune all of these systems and create a quality game.

In addition, I contributed to the development of a file-driven level loader, a spatial partitioning system for optimized collision handling, and a custom variable physics-based movement system to ensure smooth and responsive gameplay. The game was built using MonoGame in Visual Studio with C#, delivering a robust and dynamic gameplay experience.
