Shoot Em Up- Bad Branding

2D movement shooter game

Bad Branding is a fast-paced shooter where players take on the role of an outdated mascot for a popular cereal brand. The goal is to prevent rival companies from entering the office, as their presence threatens to undo your brand. Success depends on mastering the game’s challenging mechanics, including slippery movement with a jetpack and precise combat with a short-ranged gun. Every system in the game—from collision detection and movement to bullet physics—was built entirely from scratch. This project gave me the opportunity to dive deep into designing complex systems and honed my skills in object-oriented programming (OOP).
